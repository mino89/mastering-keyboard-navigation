About AccessiShop

Welcome to AccessiShop, the premier e-commerce platform designed with accessibility at its core. We believe that online shopping should be available to everyone, regardless of their abilities or the technologies they use to browse the web.

Our Commitment to Accessibility

Full Keyboard Navigation

Navigate our entire site using only your keyboard. Every interactive element is accessible via Tab, Enter, and arrow keys.

Screen Reader Optimized

Comprehensive ARIA labels, landmarks, and semantic HTML ensure perfect compatibility with all screen readers.

WCAG 2.1 AA Compliant

We exceed industry standards for web accessibility, ensuring our site meets all WCAG 2.1 Level AA requirements.

High Contrast & Zoom Support

Our design adapts to high contrast modes and supports zoom levels up to 400% without loss of functionality.

Need Help?

If you encounter any accessibility barriers while using our site, please contact our support team at accessibility@accessishop.com
